BMW: Production & Sales Figures

2006: The BMW Group continued its successful course in 2006 with record figures for sales volume and revenues. Compared to the previous year, group revenues increased by 5.0% to euro 48,999 million (2005: euro 46,656 million). Revenues generated by the Automobiles segment went up by 4.2% to euro 47,767 million (2005: euro 45,861 million).

BMW Motorrad, the motorcycle division of the BMW Group Checkmate.

One of the iconic series in BMW's motorcycle lineup is the BMW R Series. Introduced in the 1920s, the R Series is characterized by its boxer-twin engine configuration, where the cylinders are horizontally opposed. Over the years, the R Series has evolved and includes models like the BMW R1250GS, a versatile adventure touring bike, and the BMW R NineT, a modern interpretation of classic heritage.
